A nice warm welcome to Chicago...

The past year has certainly been an eventful one!

After spending the first 27 years of my life in Boston, I decided that it was time for a change. I moved to Chicago back on March 19th, 2020 and was welcomed in with a series of events that I'd call... less-than-ideal. During the first month living in here I lost my job due to a massive layoff (thanks COVID), I lost my car as a byproduct of some looting, and I lost all my rights to explore my new home when the quaratine began and we were all forced to stay inside.

But it wasn't ALL bad...

A lot of amazing things happened for me this past year too!

I got engaged to the love of my life!


I said goodbye to the Dodge Challenger and made room for my new car!
